About Me

I was born in Vietnam, a very beautiful country with amazing food. If you have the chance to visit Vietnam someday, I can guarantee you will have a really unique experience that you can not get anywhere else. The people are really friendly and the food is just top tier in my opinion. I stay in Vietnam until I turn 22 then I moved to the US with my family

In a way, my life in the US is so different from my previous life in Vietnam. There are a lot of new things for me to discover but there was also a lot of hardship at first. First, cultural differences play a big part that prevents me from fully adapting to the US lifestyle, everything is just so different from a small thing like the way you travel around to big things like how you work and study. Second, communication is also a big big problem for a lot of immigrants who start out a new life in the US, and I was also have that same problem

My solution to the problem above is that to try study again. Although. I already have a bachelor’s degree from a university in Vietnam, but I feel that need to start study again in US so that I can experience more about the culture and the people here. I start with Santa Ana community college and later transfer to Cal Poly Pomona.
